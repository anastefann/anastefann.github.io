ABOUT ME

I am a technical game designer currently studying at Breda University of Applied Sciences, where I've developed a strong technical skills, such as rapid prototyping, visual scripting, basic 3D modeling, and more. My passion lies in crafting complex and engaging systems that inspire creative problem-solving. I enjoy breaking these systems down and taking a logical approach at implementing them.

Beyond my academic pursuits, my diverse set of hobbies enhances my approach to game design. As an avid reader, I draw inspiration from various genres and storytelling techniques to improve my game narratives. Bullet journaling hones my planning and organizational skills, ensuring a systematic and efficient design process. Cooking and baking fuel my creativity, reflecting in innovative game mechanics and systems. Additionally, my love for playing games serves as both a source of enjoyment and a valuable learning experience, allowing me to critically analyze diverse genres and incorporate unique elements into my designs.

In the realm of game design, I leverage my academic foundation in Math-Computer Science to tackle complex problem-solving and programming challenges. This, combined with my hands-on experience in gaming and a keen eye for design, empowers me to approach game development with a holistic perspective. As I continue my journey in game design, I'm excited to explore new ideas, tackle challenges, and create engaging experiences that resonate with players.
